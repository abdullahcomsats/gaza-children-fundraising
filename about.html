Supporting Gaza's Children

Understanding the humanitarian crisis and how your donations make a difference

The Current Situation

Children in Gaza face unprecedented challenges including limited access to education, healthcare, clean water, and basic necessities. The ongoing crisis has left thousands of children in need of immediate humanitarian assistance.

Our fundraising efforts focus on providing direct support through established humanitarian organizations that have proven track records of delivering aid effectively and transparently to those who need it most.

Transparency Commitment: All funds raised through this campaign will be donated to verified humanitarian organizations working directly with children in Gaza.

How Your Donations Make a Difference

Education

School supplies, books, and educational materials for children

Healthcare

Medical supplies, treatments, and psychological support

Shelter

Safe housing, blankets, and basic living necessities

Clean Water

Access to clean drinking water and sanitation facilities

Fund Usage & Transparency

We believe in complete transparency about how donations are collected, tracked, and distributed.

Record Keeping

Every donation is logged with donor details, amount, and timestamp in our Excel transaction log

Progress Tracking

Real-time updates on our website showing total raised, donor count, and progress toward our goal

Direct Distribution

100% of donations go directly to established humanitarian organizations serving Gaza children
